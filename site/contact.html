What we will need

To confirm fit, send us this.

A short note covering the items below lets us route to the right Partner and confirm capacity within 48 hours. Email is fine; we do not require a formal RFI.

Pursuit details

  • Project name, location, sector, and approximate scale
  • Delivery model (IPD, CMAR, P3, Progressive DB, traditional, etc.)
  • Anticipated start date and key milestones
  • Prime consultant or contractor leading the pursuit (if known)
  • Any specific MCW personnel or experience requested

Typical response cadence

Within 24 hours — Acknowledgement and confirmation of receipt.

Within 48 hours — Confirmation of availability and a proposed VEC team for the pursuit.

Within 5 business days — Tailored capability statement or proposal outline, depending on the request.
